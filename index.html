Our Story

We always say that although we met through an online dating website, we could have easily met each other through the tight-knit network of Chicago climbing friends, some of whom we already had in common.

Be that as it may, Katherine saw Mike's profile and thought he was cute, and the fact that he was a climber tipped the scale. So, she "winked" at him. Mike saw the wink, and although he normally ignored "winks" on that site, the fact that she was cute and a climber tipped the scale, so he wrote her an email.

Our first date was the 4th of July, 2006. We immediately recognized each other as good people and quickly became friends. Fast forward 8 years, countless hours spent together traveling in cars and planes, hundreds of climbs and hikes, and nights spent under the stars, that intimate friendship grew into a deep relationship of respect, caring, and love. We're so thrilled to have our family and friends come to bless our future together.
